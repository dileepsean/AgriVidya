Explain psychrometrics for food engineering exam
Psychrometrics is the study of thermodynamic properties of moist air. Key terms: Dry Bulb Temperature (DBT), Wet Bulb Temperature (WBT), Relative Humidity (RH%), Dew point, Specific humidity (kg water/kg dry air), Enthalpy (kJ/kg). Used in spray drying, freeze drying, grain storage, cold store air conditioning. The psychrometric chart plots all these relationships visually. Want me to solve a numerical problem?

We believe that a student at ANGRAU, PJTSAU, or any state agricultural university deserves the same depth of learning support as a student at IIT or IARI. AgriVidya is our commitment to making that possible.
